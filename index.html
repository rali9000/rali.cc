    about me

    Ali is a Moroccan American student, artist, programmer, and producer based in Columbus, Ohio. Currently attending the Ohio State University, he's pursuing a degree in CSE with an art minor. Ali works with acrylic paint, oil pastel, charcoal, and digital mediums. As a producer I have many placements and have been producing since 2016, in which time I have developed my own style. I also make my own solo electronic music. This website was completely coded by me! I started programming in 2018, beginning with HTML, I have since learned much about Java in school, and CSS on my own time. If you have any questions feel free to contact me.
