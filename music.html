rali

I am an innovative and boundary-pushing breakcore artist based in Columbus, Ohio. Ever since I first started creating music in 2016 as a producer, I have been building an experimental sound and unique approach to my music. I draw inspiration from artists like The Distillers, Machine Girl, Venetian Snares, and Earl Sweatshirt. Recently, I have shifted my attention to electronic music, and I have crafted a unique sound that is dark, moody, and aggressive, yet also highly expressive.

Whether I'm producing for other artists or releasing solo material, I always try to bring a fresh and exciting energy to my work.
