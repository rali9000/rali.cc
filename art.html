rali

As an artist, I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. I started taking it seriously in 2018, and started getting into digital art alongside traditional art. I usually use charcoal, graphite, and ink; but I also use acrylic and oil paints. Most of my digital art is for song cover art, and all of my commissions so far have been for cover art.

check out my recent work.

If you would like a commission contact me.
